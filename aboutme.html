Soccer’s a huge part of my life, and I play at a pretty high level. I love the competition, the grind, and the way the game pushes me to keep getting better. Honestly, there’s nothing like the feeling of being out on the field.

Outside of soccer, I’m always down to try new things if they grab my interest. Sometimes I’ll stick with them, sometimes I won’t, but I like giving things a shot. You never really know what you’ll end up enjoying until you try.
